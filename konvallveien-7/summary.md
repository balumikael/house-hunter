## Property Description

A **three-floor townhouse** in Finstadjordet, Lørenskog, significantly upgraded with renovated bathrooms, new kitchen, and modern heating — offering solid value at the lowest asking price among prospects.

### Layout (3 floors)

- **1st Floor (49 m², BRA-i):** Open-plan living room and kitchen with entry hall.
  - **Entry:** Sliding door wardrobe with mirror fronts, code lock on front door.
  - **Kitchen:** Kvik brand, open to living room, social layout with cooktop facing dining area, ample counter space, light and dark smooth fronts, laminate countertop, composite sink, integrated cooktop, oven, fridge, wine cooler, dishwasher, downdraft carbon filter ventilation.
  - **Living room:** Through-going light, light colors, fireplace/stove with built-in wood shelf, direct access to south-facing terrace and garden.

- **2nd Floor (50 m², BRA-i):** Three bedrooms and bathroom.
  - **Bedrooms:** Largest ~13.9 m², all with space for bed/nightstand/wardrobe. Built-in sliding wardrobe in master bedroom.
  - **Bathroom (renovated 2021):** Beautiful tiles in black/brass/white, underfloor heating, downlights, wall-hung toilet, rainfall shower behind glass walls, double basin with drawer unit, mirror with integrated lighting, washing machine hookup. Built to TEK17. Invoices, compliance declarations and inspection certificates from contractors available.

- **Basement (43 m², BRA-i):** ⚠️ **NOT approved for habitation** — originally approved as secondary rooms/storage.
  - **Extra living room:** Used as living space (originally approved as bod/storage).
  - **Bathroom (upgraded 2023):** Tiled floor with skirting tiles, partly tiled/partly painted walls, dark fittings, wall-mounted toilet, shower with folding glass doors, washing machine hookup, external exhaust fan.
  - **Kitchen:** New fitted kitchen with smooth fronts, laminate top, sink, fridge/freezer, dishwasher, cooktop, oven, fire guard; carbon filter ventilation. ⚠️ Water/drain not connected on inspection day.
  - **Two storage rooms (boder):** ~300L hot water tank in one bod with waterguard.
