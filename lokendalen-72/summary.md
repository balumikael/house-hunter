## Condition Report — Issues to Watch ⚠️

### TG2 (Significant Deviations — Moderate Concern)

| Area | Issue |
|------|-------|
| **Roof covering** | Moisture marks on underside roof boards. Moisture tests showed normal values, but ongoing monitoring in rain recommended. |
| **Gutters & flashings** | No snow guards on full roof (not required when built, but risk of snow/ice falling). Gutters/downpipes aging — replacement needed eventually. |
| **Wall cladding** | Missing air gap at bottom of cladding — reduces ventilation behind panels, increasing moisture risk. Also: fasteners driven too deep, creating water-trapping depressions. |
| **Attic/roof structure** | Mold growth on underside of roof. Condensation symptoms detected. Elevated moisture readings in timber. Limited ventilation — needs investigation and remediation. |
| **Windows** | Past half of expected lifespan — higher heat loss, increased maintenance. Replacement needed eventually. |
| **Doors** | Past half of expected lifespan — same as windows. |
| **Terrace/balcony** | Railing does NOT meet building code requirements (not even the code from when it was built). Railing is loose — tighten for safety. |
| **Below-ground rooms** | Lined with organic materials (wood/gypsum) — risk construction for moisture. Damage often hidden. |
| **Interior stairs** | Railing height below current 0.9m requirement. Handrail is loose — tighten for safety. |
| **Interior doors** | Some doors rub against frames — need adjustment. |
| **Water pipes** | Corrosion on some pipes and manifolds. Pipes past half of expected lifespan — replacement needed eventually. |
| **Drain pipes** | Past half of expected lifespan — increased leak risk. |
| **Ventilation** | No air gap under doors to bathroom and laundry room — limits airflow, causes moisture buildup. |
| **Electrical system** | Simplified inspection only — may have undetected faults. Full inspection recommended. |
| **Drainage** | Foundation membrane ends above grade (should be 5cm below). Drainage system past half of expected lifespan. |
| **Foundation walls** | Diagonal cracks observed — may indicate uneven settling. Monitor for development. |
| **Terrain grading** | Insufficient slope away from foundation (<1:50 over 3m) — water may pool against walls. Grading adjustment recommended. |
| **Toilet room** | Inadequate ventilation; sanitaryware past half lifespan; creaking floor. |
| **Laundry room** | Surfaces, floor, and membrane past half lifespan. Floor drain height insufficient (<25mm). No membrane documentation. Fixtures aging. No door ventilation gap. |
